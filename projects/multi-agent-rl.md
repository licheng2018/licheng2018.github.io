# System-Level Optimization with Multi-Agent RL

Modeled multi-cell interference and resource allocation as a multi-agent optimization problem.

## Work

- Designed MADDPG and Actor-Critic frameworks for distributed power scheduling.
- Built simulation environments for large-scale communication system optimization.
- Studied coordination under partial observability.
- Focused on system-level efficiency, scalability, and dynamic resource allocation.

## Related Experience

This work connects with research on UAV-assisted 5G, LEO satellite systems, joint positioning and communication services, and communication-aware distributed scheduling.
